London Mapper — Data Deletion

Last updated: 01 July 2026

1. Summary

London Mapper does not have user accounts. You do not sign up, log in, or create a profile. The vast majority of data the app uses (your GPS location, route history, planned routes) is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers.

This page explains the very limited categories of data we do hold off-device, and how to ask for them to be deleted.

2. What data we hold off your device

CategoryWhat it isWhere it lives
Issue / feedback reports Anything you submit through the in-app "Report an issue" feature — typically a description, an optional email so we can reply, and the map coordinates of the issue. Our backend database.
Subscription record An anonymous identifier generated by RevenueCat (our subscription provider) plus your purchase receipt from Google Play. We do not see your name, email, address, or card details — Google Play handles billing directly. RevenueCat.
Support emails Any email you've sent us at the address below. Our email inbox.

3. How to request deletion

Send an email to support.londonmapper@gmail.com with the subject line "Data deletion request".

In the body, please tell us:

  • The email address you used when submitting feedback (if any).
  • The approximate date(s) you sent feedback or contacted support (if you remember).
  • For subscription data: the Google account email you used to subscribe (we'll use this to ask RevenueCat to wipe the anonymous record tied to your install).

You don't need to give a reason. We'll confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete the deletion within 30 days, in line with UK GDPR.

4. What gets deleted

  • Issue reports tied to your email or location are permanently removed from our database.
  • Subscription records in RevenueCat are anonymised and purged so we can no longer link any data back to you. Note: this does not cancel an active subscription with Google Play — to stop being billed, go to Play Store → Subscriptions and tap Cancel.
  • Support emails between you and us are deleted from our inbox.

5. What we don't need to delete (because we never had it)

  • Your GPS location history — processed only on your device, never transmitted to us.
  • Your planned routes or navigation history — never leaves your device.
  • Your name, full address, phone number, or payment card — we never collected these.

6. To delete the app and stop all future data collection

  • Uninstall London Mapper from your device.
  • Cancel any active subscription at Play Store → Subscriptions.
  • (Optional) email us as above to wipe any historical feedback or subscription data still on file.

7. Your other rights

Under UK GDPR you can also ask us for a copy of the data we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk. Use the same email address above.

For everything else, see our Privacy Policy.
